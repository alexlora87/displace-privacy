# Privacy Policy for Displace

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Overview
Displace is a lightweight desktop utility that simulates mouse movement to prevent your computer from going to sleep or activating the screensaver. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection
Displace does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The application operates entirely on your local device and does not connect to the internet.

## Local Settings
Displace saves the following preferences locally on your device using your app's local storage:
- Selected language
- Movement interval
- Movement intensity level
- Notification preferences

These settings never leave your device and are used solely to restore your preferences between sessions.

## System Permissions
Displace requires the following permissions to function:
- **Mouse control:** to simulate cursor movement
- **System tray access:** to run minimized in the background
- **Notifications:** to display a message when the application is minimized to the tray and when the activity timer finishes

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data.

## External Links
Displace includes optional donation links (Ko-fi, PayPal) that open in your default browser. These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review. Displace does not transmit any data to these services beyond the act of opening the link.

## Third-Party Services
Displace does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy
Displace is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users.

## Changes to This Policy
Any future updates to this privacy policy will be reflected in the app's Store listing.
